Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lift: AI Workout Planner. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Onboarding data: gender, height, weight, fitness goals, experience level, equipment, workout frequency, and target muscles.
  • Authentication: if you sign in with Google or continue as a guest, we store a unique user ID.
  • Workout plans & logs: AI-generated plans, completed sets/reps, timestamps, and workout duration.
  • Usage analytics: anonymized events to improve app performance and features.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate personalized workout plans via AI.
  • To save and retrieve your workout history securely (locally for guests, cloud for signed-in users).
  • To track your progress and provide workout recommendations.
  • To analyze app usage and improve functionality.

4. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except for:

  • OpenAI, for generating workout content (under their privacy terms).
  • Firebase, for authentication and data storage.

All data is stored securely with Firebase Firestore for cloud users and locally on your device for guest users. We use SSL/TLS for all network transfers.

5. Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your profile or workout data at any time by contacting us at simodigitalgaency@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll post the new version here with the "Last updated" date.

If you have any questions, please email simodigitalgaency@gmail.com.
